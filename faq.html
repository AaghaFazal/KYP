Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Kushal Yuva Program (KYP) — common questions and answers.

KYP is a skill development program launched by the Bihar Government to improve youth employability through training in communication skills, life skills, and basic computer knowledge.
Any Bihar resident aged around 15–28 years who has passed at least Class 10 can apply. Age relaxation is available for reserved categories.
Yes, the training is provided free of cost by the government (for most eligible candidates).
The program covers:
  • Communication skills (English & Hindi)
  • Life/soft skills
  • Basic computer and IT skills
The total training duration is approximately 240 hours (around 3 months).
Yes, you will receive an official certificate from Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM).
You can apply online through the BSDM or 7 Nishchay Yuva portal and complete verification at your nearest DRCC (District Registration & Counseling Center).
Yes, KYP training centers are available across all districts of Bihar.
KYP focuses on skill development and employability. While it does not guarantee a job, it greatly improves job opportunities.
Yes, both male and female candidates are encouraged to apply.
