About Kushal Yuva Program (KYP)

The Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM) has launched a unique skill training programme by the name of "Kushal Yuva Program" which would enhance the employability Skills of all aspirants in the age group of 15-28 years.

Training comprises of Life skills, Communication Skills (English & Hindi) and Basic computer literacy which acts as a value add to domain specific training.
